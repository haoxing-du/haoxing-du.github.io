About Research Misc

Haoxing Du | 杜浩星

I’m a PhD student in theoretical physics at UC Berkeley. Prior to Berkeley, I was fortunate to be part of the Perimeter Scholars International program, and studied at Harvey Mudd College.

My current research interests are quantum foundations and quantum information theory.
